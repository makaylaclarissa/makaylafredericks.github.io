About Me

My name is Makayla Fredericks. I am originally from Harlem, New York but was raised in The Bronx. I am a sophomore at Fordham University in Lincoln Center. I am a double major in Journalism and Communications and Culture with a minor in Marketing. One of my passions is music and because of that, I plan to pursue music journalism an interview different music artists and help them tell their story. Part of how I was able to do this was through The "Problem" With Diversity project. This project taught me that there isn't just one medium you can use to tell a story. I am excited to take what I've learned from that project to further my career.
